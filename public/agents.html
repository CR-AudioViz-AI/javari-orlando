Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to join the partner program?

No! Our partner program is completely free. You only pay when you make a booking, and that cost is passed to your client.

How and when do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via direct deposit or check, your choice. You'll receive payment around the 15th of each month for the previous month's completed stays.

Do I need any certifications?

While not required, we encourage partners to have relevant certifications (CLIA, ASTA, etc.) or a host agency affiliation. We accept both independent agents and agency employees.

Can I use your tools on my own website?

Yes! Our white-label widgets can be embedded on your website with your branding. This is available to partners with 25+ annual bookings.
