üí° Passholder Pro Tips

üéØ Make Reservations Early

Passholders can only hold 3 park reservations at a time. Book popular dates as soon as you use one up! Park Pass availability can be limited, especially for Magic Kingdom.

üí≥ Use Payment Plans

Disney offers monthly payment plans with a down payment. Spread the cost over 12 months! Note: You must maintain an active pass to keep benefits.

üì± Check the App Daily

Passholder-exclusive offerings, merchandise, and experiences pop up in the My Disney Experience app. Check the "Passholder" section regularly!

üéÅ Stack Discounts Carefully

Usually can't combine AP discount with other offers, BUT you can use Disney gift cards bought at 5% off from Target RedCard!
