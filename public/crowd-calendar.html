January Low After Jan 1 holidays, one of the slowest times. Marathon weekend (mid-Jan) can spike MK crowds.

March Very Busy Spring Break season. Schools across the country are out. One of the busiest months.
April Busy Spring Break continues early April. Easter week is packed. Calms down after.

August Moderate Schools start returning. Crowds decrease late August. Still hot.
September Low One of the quietest months. Hurricane season. EPCOT Food & Wine begins.
October Moderate Halloween events draw crowds. Columbus Day weekend busy. Weather improving.
November Busy Thanksgiving week is very crowded. Early Nov is quieter. Holiday decorations up.
