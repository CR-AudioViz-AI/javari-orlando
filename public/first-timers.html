üè∞ 2. Disney World 101

4 Theme Parks

  • Magic Kingdom: Classic Disney, Cinderella Castle, best for all ages
  • EPCOT: Future World + World Showcase, great food & drink
  • Hollywood Studios: Star Wars, Toy Story, thrill rides
  • Animal Kingdom: Safari, Avatar, nature-themed

Must-Know Terms

  • Lightning Lane: Skip-the-line passes ($15-35/ride)
  • Park Hopper: Visit multiple parks same day
  • ADR: Advance Dining Reservations (book 60 days out)
  • Early Entry: Resort guests enter 30 min early

  • ‚Ä¢ Book dining 60 days ahead or you'll miss popular restaurants
  • ‚Ä¢ One park per day is usually enough (don't rush!)

üé¢ 3. Universal Orlando 101

3 Theme Parks

  • Universal Studios: Movies, Harry Potter Diagon Alley
  • Islands of Adventure: Thrill rides, Hogwarts, VelociCoaster
  • Epic Universe: NEW in 2025! Nintendo, Harry Potter Ministry

Must-Know Terms

  • Express Pass: Skip-the-line (included at Premier hotels)
  • Park-to-Park: Required for Hogwarts Express
  • Early Park Admission: Hotel guests enter 1 hour early

üí° Universal vs Disney

  • ‚Ä¢ Universal has MORE thrilling rides (more coasters)

  • ‚Ä¢ Harry Potter fans: Wizarding World is at Universal!
