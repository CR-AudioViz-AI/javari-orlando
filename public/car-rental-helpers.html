‚úàÔ∏è Orlando Airport Pickup Tips

üõ¨ MCO - Orlando International Airport

The main airport for Disney & Universal. Car rental center is a short train ride from the terminal.

  • On-site companies: Enterprise, Hertz, Avis, Budget, National, Alamo, Dollar, Thrifty
  • Off-site (shuttle): Sixt, Fox, Payless, ACE - usually cheaper!
  • Peak times: Expect 30-60 min wait Sat/Sun mornings

üõ©Ô∏è SFB - Orlando Sanford Airport

Smaller airport ~45 min north. Used by Allegiant, some international charters.

  • Fewer options: Hertz, Avis, Budget, Enterprise, Alamo, National
  • Pros: Less crowded, faster pickup
  • Cons: Longer drive to Disney (~1 hour)
