üí∞ Save Money on Lightning Lane

Skip It on Low Crowd Days

In January, September, or early February, crowds are low enough that rope drop + good strategy = no need for LL.

Rope Drop the Headliners

Arrive 45min early. Ride TRON, Rise, or Flight of Passage at rope drop for FREE instead of $20+ per person.

Use Single Rider Lines

Millennium Falcon, Test Track, Everest, and Rock 'n' Roller Coaster have free single rider lines that are often faster than LL.

Only Buy What You'll Use

Don't buy Multi Pass for Animal Kingdom (only a few LL rides) or your last park day when energy is low.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy Lightning Lane at the park?

Yes, but the most popular rides sell out before noon. For headliners like Rise of Resistance or TRON, you MUST book at 7am or they'll be gone.

What's the difference between Multi Pass and Single Pass?

Multi Pass ($15-35/day) gives unlimited LL access to 40+ rides. Single Pass ($15-35/ride) is for 5 specific headliners not included in Multi Pass.

Can I park hop with Lightning Lane?

Yes! Multi Pass works at all 4 parks. You can book rides at different parks throughout the day. Single Pass is per ride regardless of which park.

Is it worth it for kids?

Kids under 3 are free. For kids 3+, you pay full price. If your kids are tall enough for the major rides, yes it's worth it. If they're only riding kiddie rides, skip it.
